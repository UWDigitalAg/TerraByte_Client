Please do not give our data (whole or in part) to others directly. 
We ask that you refer them to the portal to download the data. 

Portal link: https://terrabyte.acs.uwinnipeg.ca/resources.html#tools 

The data is offered "as is" and in the context of researching its possibilities. 
We spend a continuous effort on to provide the data that is as good as possible. 
You can encounter images in the dataset that are bugged, for example, 
seeing fragments of other plants sticking into the frame. 

Please cite the following paper for any publications or presentations based on our data.
Beck MA, Liu CY, Bidinosti CP, Henry CJ, Godee CM, et al. (2020) 
An embedded system for the automated generation of labeled plant 
images to enable machine learning applications in agriculture. 
PLOS ONE 15(12): e0243923. https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0243923
